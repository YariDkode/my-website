About Us

We are a married veteran couple navigating college life while living full-time in our Lance travel trailer. “Rolling Through School” is our way of sharing what it’s like to balance coursework, travel, and the everyday adventures that come with RV living.

With our service dog, Liz, by our side, every day is a mix of learning and exploring. From quiet mornings studying by the mountains to long hikes on weekends, this journey is about chasing both our degrees and the freedom of the open road.

Our Story

We are both proud Navy veterans who first met while serving. The military took us on separate paths for a while with long underways and time apart because of orders. Now, embracing the freedom of the road lets us stay together, explore new places, and make up for the years we spent apart.
